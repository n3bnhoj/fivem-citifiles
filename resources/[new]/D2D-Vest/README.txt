To install:

1) Upload the SQL to your database.

2) Add "D2D-Vest" to your resource folder

3) Add "D2D-Vest" to your server.cfg

4) Resart server


To use:

1) Equip vest to "use" in your inventory

2) Press "E" to detonate 


To defuse:

1) Equip vest

2) Equip bomb defuser to "use" in your inventory
